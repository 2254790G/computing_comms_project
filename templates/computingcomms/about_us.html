Whether you're studying Computing Science at Univeristy of Glasgow or you're just interested in Computing, ComputingComms is exactly what you were looking for. It is platform to discuss course questions and test yourself on relevant material. There is a variety of quizzes on all available computing science classes for that year and a forum for those to upload and discuss fragments of code. Moreover, lecturers can access and assist those having problems by providing sample code, emailing individual students directly or posting hints to the forum. It's a place for students to feel more at ease to ask questions and test their knowledge.?
